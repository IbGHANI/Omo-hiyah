The Directress of

THE DIRECTRESS Name: Hajia Fatimah Khalilullahi Soro 2 She was born on the 10th of October 1965 to the family of Alhaji Soliu Omolabi and Alhaja Hajarah Omolabi. Her late father was a native of Oke Oyi in the Ilorin east area of Kwara state, he was a renowned livestock trader while her mother was a successful trader. EARLY LIFE AND EDUCATION Hajia Fatimah Khalilullahi Soro 2 started her primary education in 1980 at Okesuna Primary School Ilorin. She then proceeded to ST. Clare's Grammar School, Offa, for her secondary education. As her husband prefers her to be a teacher, she proceeded for two years of teacherâ€™s training in Ilorin. Then, she did the NCE program at Ilorin Kwara State College of education, where she studied Primary Education & Islamic Studies. Thereafter, she bagged her Bachelor of degree in educational management from Ekiti State University. Also, she possessed many professional qualifications in the field of education as well as numerous certificates of participation in seminars, conferences, symposiums, summits and the like. From her educational experience, Hajiyah Fatimah has tremendously contributed and is contributing to the development of the Omo Hiya Group of Schools. She has been playing the role of a teacher, an administratress, a councillor as well as a mother both in the school and at home. From her pool of knowledge, she has excelled in introducing fantastic teaching and learning skills in the school and has well as able to elevate Omo Hiya to the degree that it can compete with others in society.
