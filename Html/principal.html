The Principal

THE SCHOOL PRINCIPAL
BIOGRAPHY OF KAMALDEEN AJUFUNBAYA OLAITAN Kamaldeen Ajufunbaya Olaitan was born into the family of Alhaji Ibrahim Ajufunbaya and Alhaja Suebat Ajufunbaya in the late 80s. He started his elementary education at Oke Aluko LGEA School, Ilorin from where he proceeded to Okelele Secondary School before completing his secondary education at Community Secondary School, Ogundele Alaho, Ibadan, in 2004. In 2005, He got admission to study Banking and Finance at Kwara State Polytechnic, Ilorin where he bagged his Ordinary National Diploma certificate in 2008. Between 2008 and 2009, he enrolled as an Industrial Training student at Balogun Gambari Microfinance Bank where his experience as a banker began. His zeal for knowledge made him put in for his Higher National Diploma in Banking and Finance also at Kwara State Polytechnic, Ilorin in 2009. By 2011, he was certified with Higher National Diploma in Banking and Finance. His service year was between 2011 and 2012 in Jigawa State where he learnt the Hausa language at an average level. He joined Omo hiya Group of School as a bursar and subject teacher in 2015 and later rose to the position of Vice-Principal after some years of remarkable service to the development of the school. He is currently the principal of the citadel of learning, Omo hiya Group of School and has recorded tremendous success since he was appointed the school head on the 18th of January, 2021. As a student of social sciences, he never let waste his marketing prowess as he used to be a marketer for the popular dealer in iced fish -Asake Fishery. Presently, he is an undergraduate student at Kwara State University, Malete where he would be bagging his Bachelor of Sciences in Finance in a couple of months. He is happily married with children.
