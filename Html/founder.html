ABOUT

The Founder of

Name And Genealogy:
Sheikh Ibrahim Khalilullahi Soro 2 (Proprietor of Omo Hiya Group of Schools, Imam Omo Hiya Central mosque, Chief Missional JAOZIAN, Islamic preacher). Sheikh was born in 1960 to the (late) Sheikh Abubakar bin Mustapha Omo Hiya al-Barnawi and Alhaja Hawwau Soro, both of Soro’s compounds Gambari area, Ilorin East area of Kwara state. His father was a prominent and pious scholar while his mother was a full housewife who dedicated her whole life to the service of her husband. EARLY LIFE AND PURSUIT FOR KNOWLEDGE Sheikh soro 2 started Quranic school at the age of 4 years under the tutelage of his father. By 1970, he had completed the reading of the Glorious Qur'an and memorization of some portions therein. Sheikh continued his pursuit of knowledge by starting rudiment knowledge in Arabic and Islamic studies under his father till the latter was demised in 1986. Thereafter, he continued learning under his elder brother (eldest son of Sheikh Omo Hiya Soro) in person of Sheikh Mu'mun Abubakar Soro, who taught him different disciplines such as Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence), Adab (Arabic Literature), Nahw (Arabic Grammer) Sarf (Morphology), and many ancient books, etc. Ustaz soro 2 also studied under many scholars such as the late Sheikh Farooq Omo Hiya Soro (his elder brother), Sheikh Adebayo, and many more. During his childhood, Sheikh Soro 2 assisted his mother in hawking and other house chores. At his youth age, he dedicated his time to the service of his father who had become old by bathing him, always keeping his company, keeping his property and he had never taken a dime from it. Due to his restless service, Allāh blessed and rewarded him right from his tender age. He got married to 2 wives (Alhaja Habibah and Alhaja Fatimah) and was blessed with 10 children as well as grandchildren. May Allah preserve them all. HONOURS AND AWARDS Being a notable scholar, Islamic preacher, and religious leader, Ustaz Soro 2 has contributed immensely to the spread, growth, and development of Islām, he has been exemplary for others due to his vastness in knowledge, wisdom, humbleness, kindness, friendliness, trustworthiness, righteousness coupled with generosity. Based on his unwavering achievements, scholastic excellence, and positive impact on the lives of the masses, many associations, societies, movements, both educational and non-educational organizations at the local and national level, conferred him numerous awards such as: i. 'Fakhru' l Ulama''(honoured by Al-Huda Islamic Foundation) ii. 'Religious Adviser' (honoured by At-Taqwah Islamic Foundation) iii. ‘A pride of the community’ (honoured by Markaz Najeebuddeen) and other associations like: Al-Qowafy Islamic Awareness Networking, ii. Ilorin Emirates Student’s Union, iii. Nuru Muhammad School of Arabic and Islamic studies iv. Jam'iyyatu Ahbabi Nabiyy Mustapha (S.A.W) v. An-Najeeb Islamic Foundation etc. Today, Sheikh Khalilullahi Soro 2 is the founder of Omo Hiya Group of Schools, the Imam of Omo Hiya central mosque, the Chief Founder and Missional of Jaozian, a Preacher and a Religious Adviser. We beseech Allāh to preserve him for the humanities with the longevity of life and sound health. AMEEN.
